Get ready, Dune heads, because we're traveling way back in time for Max's “Dune: Prophecy.” Set 10,000 years before Paul Atreides' arrival on Arrakis, the prequel series follows two Harkonnen sisters as their battle to protect humanity from outside threats leads to the formation of the Bene Gesserit. Devoted fans of the novels take note, the show's story is inspired by Brian Herbert and Kevin J. Anderson's novel “Sisterhood of Dune,” rather than a direct adaptation.
